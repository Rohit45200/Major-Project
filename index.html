Welcome to My College

About Sengunthar Engineering College

Sengunthar Engineering College was established in 2001 with the goal of achieving excellence in engineering and technological pursuits. It is affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education).

Educational Philosophy

The college aims to redefine education by addressing the educational gap among rural students in India. It focuses on instilling a strong work ethic, discipline, and principles that provide students with a competitive edge.

Key Features

  • Enhanced hands-on training and well-established infrastructure.
  • Updated software and access to a comprehensive library & digital library.
  • MoUs with reputed organizations for academic collaboration.
  • Industrial visits, guest lectures, workshops, symposiums, and exhibitions.
  • Focus on sports, games, and co/extracurricular activities.

Faculty and Support

Our dedicated faculty includes knowledgeable and professional educators who inspire students. Leadership from the Director and Principal ensures every student receives guidance and support.

Student Development

Each student is monitored closely with constant reviews and personal counselling. This approach helps students to excel in academics and life.

Global Impact

The college is committed to producing globally impactful talents who blossom and spread their fragrance across the world.

Summary

Sengunthar Engineering College emphasizes holistic student development through excellent education, hands-on training, industry collaboration, and individual attention in a vibrant and supportive learning environment.

Location

Sengunthar Engineering College and Snegunthar College of Engineering are located at a distance of 3 kms from Tiruchengode on the Tiruchengode-Namakkal State Highway at Kosavampalayam with a sprawling 60 acres panoramic landscape and near the foothill of Nagagiri from where Lord Arthenareeswara showers His blessing to His devotees. Tiruchengode, a renowned entrepreneurialtown is well connected to the important cities like Erode (20Kms), Salem (45Kms), Namakkal (35Kms) and Karur (55Kms)..

Professional Associations

Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE)

The leading national professional society for technical education, ISTE aims at the career development of teachers and personality development of students. It partners with AICTE and MHRD to run refinement programs and boasts a wide membership base across institutions including IITs and NITs.

Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE India)

An affiliate of SAE International, SAEINDIA promotes advancement in the mobility sector, organizing international exhibitions covering automotive, aerospace, and commercial vehicle technology. It connects engineers, academics, and industry leaders across the country.

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

The world‚Äôs largest professional association with 400,000+ members globally, IEEE fosters innovation and technical advancement in electrical, electronics, computer engineering, and telecommunications sectors.

Computer Society of India (CSI)

India‚Äôs first and largest body of computer professionals, CSI is a non-profit organization that promotes computer science and technology through seminars, workshops, and member networking across 72 chapters and 500+ student branches.

Indian Concrete Institute (ICI)
